Hi! I am a Research Scientist at NVIDIA Research working with Prof. Song Han and Dr. Enze Xie. I received my Ph.D. from King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) under the supervision of Prof. Jürgen Schmidhuber. My research studies multimodal generative models, with a particular focus on image and video generation. More broadly, I am interested in understanding and exploiting multi-step inference dynamics in neural networks, with the long-term goal of building Physical AI systems capable of reasoning about and interacting with the physical world.
